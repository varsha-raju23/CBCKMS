Getting Started

How do I create a TunnelKMS account? â–¼
Click "Create Account" on the login page. Enter your email and password, select your role (Viewer/Engineer/Manager/Admin), and agree to terms. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account before logging in.
Why do I need to complete my profile? â–¼
Profile completion is required to access the dashboard. Your profile information helps your team identify who uploaded documents, and ensures documents are properly attributed to the right department and company. Fill in your name, mobile, company details, and department.
I didn't receive a verification email. What do I do? â–¼
First, check your spam/junk folder. If not found, go to the login page, enter your email, and click "Resend verification email". If issues persist, contact your system administrator to check the email configuration.

Document Management

What file types can I upload? â–¼
TunnelKMS supports: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PPT, PPTX, TXT, CSV, DWG (AutoCAD), DXF, and images (JPG, PNG, GIF). Maximum file size is 50MB. For AutoCAD drawings, the AI will not be able to extract text but the files can still be stored and downloaded.
How are documents stored and organized? â–¼
Every uploaded document receives a unique Document ID (format: TKM-XXXXXXXX). Documents are organized by Project Name, Department, Document Type, and Tunnel Section. You can search and filter using any of these fields on the Documents page.
Can I delete uploaded documents? â–¼
Engineers and Managers can delete their own uploaded documents. Admins can delete any document. Deleted documents are soft-deleted (marked as deleted) and won't appear in listings. Admins can permanently delete from the Admin Panel.
How does document search work? â–¼
The search uses MongoDB full-text search across document names, project names, descriptions, extracted text content, tunnel section, and tags. For best results, use specific keywords related to the tunnel project, location, or document type.

AI Assistant

How does the AI assistant work? â–¼
The AI assistant uses Google Gemini and is trained only on your uploaded tunnel construction documents. When you ask a question, it searches through the text content extracted from your PDFs, Word documents, and text files, then generates an answer with document references. It will not answer from general knowledge — only from your uploaded files.
Why does the AI say "information not available in uploaded documents"? â–¼
This means the answer was not found in any of the uploaded documents. Try uploading relevant documents first (e.g., safety reports, design specs). Also ensure documents are in text-extractable format (PDF with text, not scanned images). The AI only answers from your document database.
How do I get better AI answers? â–¼
Upload comprehensive documents (PDFs with searchable text, Word docs). Be specific in your questions — include project name, tunnel section, or date range. Ask one question at a time. The more documents you upload, the better the AI's knowledge base becomes.

Roles & Permissions

What are the different user roles? â–¼
Viewer: Can view and download documents, use AI assistant.
Engineer: All Viewer permissions + can upload documents.
Manager: All Engineer permissions + can manage team documents.
Admin: Full access including Admin Panel, user management, and permanent deletion.

Security

How is my data secured? â–¼
TunnelKMS uses industry-standard security: JWT tokens for authentication, bcrypt password hashing (12 rounds), rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks, CORS protection, Helmet.js security headers, and MongoDB secured with authentication. All API routes are protected and require valid tokens.
